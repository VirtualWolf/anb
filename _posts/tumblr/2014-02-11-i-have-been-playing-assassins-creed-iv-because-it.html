I have been playing Assassin’s Creed IV because it came for free with my graphics card.

When I get into combat with guards, if I get far enough away from them I can hide in a haystack.

Sometimes they get suspicious and prod the haystack with a sword, but when I’m hiding, I can one-shot them

So I end up in a kind of infinite loop - guard gets suspicious, prods haystack, I strangle them and pull their body into the haystack. New guard hears something, comes to investigate, prods haystack… etc.

I don’t know how many bodies are in that haystack now

—Jirra
