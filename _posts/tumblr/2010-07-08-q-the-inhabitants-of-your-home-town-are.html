Q: The inhabitants of your home town are inexorably turning into evil agents of the undead. You are given the task of putting them out of their misery. Do you:

  1. Put your zombie loved ones to the sword.
  2. Tell your commanding officer he has crossed a terrible threshold, and leave him to do the job himself.
  3. Hope the secret passage still works.

A: 3.
