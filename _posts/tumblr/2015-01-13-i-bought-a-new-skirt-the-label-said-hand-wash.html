I bought a new skirt. The label said, “HAND WASH ONLY, COLD WATER, DO NOT TUMBLE-DRY”. So I chucked it into the washing machine and then tumble-dried it. The skirt is fine but the label is now unreadable.
—Jirra, demonstrating truth in advertising.
